About us

Io mangio, meaning "i eat" in Italian, is a family owned restaurant in the heart of Stockholm. In the midst of the financial crisis of the late 90's, founder Generic di Genericnàmo wanted to bring joy to the residents of Stockholm and started Io Mangio togheter with his wife Non Parlé Italiano. Generic is educated in Palermo and has, in his food journey, perfected recipes from Milan in the north, to Naples in the south. In late 2000 Generic and Non Parlé invested in a wineyard in Bologna to create a delicious wine that you can drink togheter with our extraordinary menu, please ask the waiter for a wine and food recommendation.
